About Us :-

A leading oil extraction and manufacturing company. We extract vegetable and cosmetic oils from cotton seeds. We also manufacture candles, soaps and more. Our products are of high quality That's because we produce according to quality and safety standards. We offer the best and safest for you.
The factory area is about 5307 square meter in hash. The area of ​​our lands planted with cotton in Al-Nakhil area in Al-Wadmani city is about 10 acres. We are keen to follow the stages of oil production, starting from the cultivation stage until the stages of packaging and delivery.

Our vision :-

To become the first brand in Sudan In the manufacture of cotton oils

Our goals :-

To work on raising the levels of agriculture and manufacturing and using the latest technologies to make the most of cotton seeds

Our message :-

Providing high-value and effective products at a competitive cost by a specialized team that works and maintains performance levels in the field of safety, environmental protection and consumer protection.

Our Core Values :-

Excellence: We strive for excellence in production, in packaging, in packaging, in every work we do Teamwork: We strive to work in a team spirit Integrity: We conduct our business according to the highest ethical standards.
